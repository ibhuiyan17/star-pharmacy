At Star Pharmacy, we are dedicated to provide quality services that go beyond just filling prescriptions. We strive to empower each of our unique patients with the ability to be in control of their pharmaceutical needs.

Consultation

One-on-one or group consultation services are always available by our well-trained Pharmacist. We are happy to sit down with you and address any concerns you have and can thoroughly review your current and previous medications.

Free Delivery

We offer free, speedy delivery on a daily basis. Our service is fast and very convenient. Simply request a delivery option

Key Services

- We accept most insurance plans

- Easy prescription transfers

- Refill reminders for your medication

- Prescription ready in 10-15 minutes

MTM Services

Our Pharmacy is certified to provide distinct medication therapy management service for our patients with hypertension, higher cholesterol, asthma, diabetes, congestive heart failure (CHF), and allergies to ensure the best therapeutic outcomes.

Deals and Offers

We offer competitive prices in the community and will match & beat any competitor price! New or transferred prescriptions eligible to receive special offers. We also offer free health screenings.

Special Items

We carry items not commonly stocked by in-person pharmacies such as halal vitamins or Veterinary medicine at low costs.
